about calm coffee

I came up with the idea for this blog right after going decaf to help with my anxiety. I am a huge coffee fan. I drank a ton, and I work in downtown Denver, which means I was drinking the good stuff. I'd gotten to where I'd drive across town for a specific cup. I was pretty disappointed at having to miss out, but I noticed some shops had decaf on the menu. I respected these shops and gave them a try. It was great stuff. None of my research had unearthed these places as great for decaf. Most listed "top-10 best decaf coffees" but they weren't really up to par for the most part. This blog aims to change that. It's going to chronicle my journey as I seek out great shops and great experiences while also drinking decaf. I'm sure things will change and evolve as I learn more and things move along, but one thing will stay the same, a positive focus on places and brands that provide great decaf coffee. That means I probably won't be writing negative reviews, I'll just not review those places. This blog should show folks where to go, so that's what I'll focus on.
